In a galaxy far, far away... everyone is a DevOps engineer, and they need your help.

You are invited to participate in a Hackathon which imagines a world where resilience is the key to survival.

Participants will pitch their ideas, self-select teams, and spend 10 hours creating an application that improves resilience of applications at Ford.

House Rules

  • Come in ready to HACK!
  • Think differently.
  • Be prepared with your laptops, chargers, and obtain necessary accesses ahead of time (github).
  • Leverage the diverse skills of a balanced team.
  • Pairing is encouraged!
  • Utilize shortcuts and take advantage of tools like /dev/central/station/
  • Make use of APIs and open source software.
  • Keep track of time, so that you can deliver a product by the end of the event.
  • Have fun!

Judging

Each team will have 5 minutes to showcase their product and present their learnings to the group.

In addition the First Place prize, teams will have the chance to win the People’s Choice Award.
