IN A GALAXY FAR, FAR AWAY... API producers and API consumers are gathering at API Marketplace to build the Resilence, by API and for API so API shall not perish from the Galaxy.

You are invited to participate in a Hackathon which imagines a world where APIs are the key to survival.

Participants will pitch their ideas, self-select teams, and spend 10 hours creating, enhancing, and utilizing APIs at Ford.

House Rules

  • Everyone is encouraged to submit ideas. Teams will be pre-formed based on challenges and ideas via an open office hour and online tools.
  • Team sizes between 3-7 people are recommended.
  • Extensive preparation and coding ahead of the hackathon is discouraged. Ideation is okay!
  • Leverage the diverse skills of a balanced team. Extra points will be granted to teams with diverse background (ITO, MPP, FMCC, etc...) and role (Product Manager, Software Engineer, Product Designer, Architect, etc...)
  • Utilize shortcuts and take advantage of tools like /dev/central/station/
  • Be prepared with your laptops, chargers, and obtain necessary accesses ahead of time (github).
  • Make use of APIs and open source software.
  • Keep track of time, so that you can deliver a product by the end of the event.
  • Pairing is encouraged!
  • Have fun!

Judging

Each team will have 5 minutes to showcase their product and present their learnings to the group.

In addition the First Place prize, teams will have the chance to win the Ready to Ship, Most Creative, Best Business Case, and People’s Choice awards.

IT Director's Challenge Award: IT Director will propose a special challenge and select a winning team that best develops a special solution to meet the challenge.
